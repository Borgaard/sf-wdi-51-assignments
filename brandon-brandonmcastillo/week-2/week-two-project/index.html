Our Mission

To accommodate the busy life of San Francisco, we have acquired a team of dedicated chefs and drivers in order to serve you traditional Japanese cuisine on the go. Gozai Sushi opened in 2019 and is currently one of the hottest sushi delivery services in the Bay Area. We value quality over quantity compared to other sushi restaurants. We hold our values and traditions to heart. Our menu is small, but very powerful. Every item is cooked, seared and crafted to perfection by top chefs in the business. We strive for excellence.
