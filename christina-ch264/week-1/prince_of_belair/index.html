My Life & Times

By Willard Carroll Smith

In West Philadelphia, born and raised, on a playground is where I spent most of my days:

  • Chilling out
  • Maxing
  • Relaxing, all cool

I was shooting some b-ball outside of the school when a couple of guys — they were up to no good — started making trouble in the neighbourhood.

I got in one little fight and my mum got scared. She said,

"You'll move in with your Auntie and your Uncle in Bel-Air."
