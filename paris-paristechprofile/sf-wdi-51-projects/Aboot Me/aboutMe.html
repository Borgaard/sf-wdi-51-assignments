Who Am I?

I think there's an artist hidden in the bottom of every single one of us. The shadows are just like the highlights, but we're going in the opposite direction. The only prerequisite is that it makes you happy. If it makes you happy then it's good. A beautiful little sunset. These little son of a guns hide in your brush and you just have to push them out. Anyone can paint. Nice little fluffy clouds laying around in the sky being lazy. These trees are so much fun. I get started on them and I have a hard time stopping. Now we can begin working on lots of happy little things. Poor old tree. Just use the old one inch brush. Just go back and put one little more happy tree in there. We'll do another happy little painting.
