About Me

My name is Amberly and I am an aspiring web developer. I studied math and art, which are my two passions in life. To me, there are only a few things that are more satisfying than solving a complex problem. In my spare time, I enjoy practicing my makeup skills, playing video games, and hanging out with my family. I am a great lover of music as well, and I sang in my college choir for four years.

Some of my other pastimes include watching sports. I am a great fan of ice hockey and I cheer for the San Jose Sharks. I also love to watch movies and television shows. Although I am always ready for a good new show, I still enjoy reruns of The Office and catching up on Game of Thrones. My current favorite shows are The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel, The Good Place, and Steven Universe.
